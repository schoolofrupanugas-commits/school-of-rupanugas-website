About School of Rupanugas

Serving ISKCON's Preaching Mission with Authentic Training and Bhakti Confidence

Our purpose is to empower practitioners and temple leaders through time-honored training rooted in Shastric knowledge and practical preaching skills.

To equip devotees with practical preaching and teaching skills with philosophical understanding to serve ISKCON's preaching mission effectively.

Developing strategic skills for effective preaching and organizational leadership.

Conducting courses based on authentic teaching methods and Shastric knowledge.

Serving the training needs of temples and leaders for their congregations.

Adapting training methods according to time, place, and circumstances.

Inspiring individuals with unwavering confidence on the path of Bhakti Yoga.

In the Service of Srila Prabhupad
